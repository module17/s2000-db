# Differential Fluid Change DIY

### Parts

- Fill bolt crush washer No. 90402-PCZ-003
- Drain bolt crush washer No. 90401-PCZ-003
- 1 quart | 75W-90 Gear oil


### Tools

- 24mm socket
- Ratchet 1/2" drive
- Torque wrench
- Fluid pump
- Oil drain pan
- Rubber mallet
- Floor jack
- Jack stands
- Wheel chocks

### Torque Specs

- Drain bolt - 33 lb-ft
- Fill bolt - 33 lb-ft
