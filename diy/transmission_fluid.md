# Transmission Fluid Change DIY

### Parts

- Fill bolt crush washer
No. 94109-20000

- Drain bolt crush washer
No. 94109-14000

- 2 quarts - OEM MTF Fluid

### Tools

- 17 socket
- Ratchet 3/8" drive
- Torque wrench
- Fluid pump
- Oil drain pan
- Rubber mallet
- Floor jack
- Jack stands
- Wheel chocks

### Torque Specs

- Drain bolt - 29 lb-ft
- Fill bolt - 33 lb-ft
